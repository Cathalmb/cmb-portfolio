Bia Energy

Scope UX/UI, Art Direction, Design System / Client Bia Energy

Building a brand from nothing for Ireland's largest anaerobic digestion plant — strategy, identity, digital, and launch.

/ The Challenge

A blank sheet. One goal. An industry to disrupt.

Bia Energy came to us with no brand, no identity, and no public presence. Entering a competitive clean energy market, they had a powerful story, but no visual language to tell it. The challenge was precise: craft an identity that balanced trust and innovation without tipping into corporate blankness or greenwashing. The brand had to work in a hi-vis jacket, on a conference stage, and in a B2B sales conversation, simultaneously.

/ The Solution

Strategy before design. Every time.

We opened with user persona research and a semi-fictional archetype model to map Bia Energy's B2B audience; demographics, psychographics, behaviours. This fed a full client discovery workshop covering voice, values, and positioning. Identity work began only once the strategic foundation was set.

001 Simplicity & clarity A design language that communicates at a glance, in any context.
002 Recognisable yet distinctive Standing apart in a competitive sector without being loud.
003 Scalable & future-proof Built to flex as the company grows into new markets and products.

/ What we delivered

Everything. From first brief to launch day.

This was an end-to-end engagement, one of those rare projects where we touched every layer of the brand's public presence.

  • Brand Identity & Guidelines
  • Content Creation
  • Marketing Collateral
  • Custom WordPress Build
  • Carbon Calculator
  • Launch Event
  • AV & Photography
  • Conference Materials

The website required a bespoke WordPress theme — purpose-built, not templated — alongside a custom carbon offset calculator allowing B2B clients to model their own savings. The launch event brought the brand to market with a fully coordinated keynote, mailing campaign, and AV production.

/ Outcomes

A brand built to lead,
not follow.

001 Market-ready from day one Brand system deployed across digital, print, environmental, and event touchpoints simultaneously at launch.
002 Built to scale Scalable identity system designed to flex with new energy products, markets, and partnerships without a rebrand.
003 Bespoke B2B tool Custom carbon calculator embedded in the website — a direct lead generation asset for the commercial sales team.
004 Full lifecycle delivered From blank-sheet brief to live brand, website, and public launch — managed end-to-end by one creative team.
